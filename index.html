About TUYU

A music unit consisting of Pusu on guitar and in charge of composing
the songs, Rei as vocals, and miro on piano. The group was formed on June
12, 2019. Its name comes from the Japanese word for “rainy season”
during which it was formed.They're constantly ranked in both Japanese
and foreign music distribution sites and appear in their popular playlists.
A group of artists to keep an eye on with many fans around the world.

On May 31st, 2024, Pusu (ぷす), also known as Yano Asaya (矢野麻也),
was arrested for the attempted murder of a teenage girl he was dating.
According to the Metropolitan Police Department, a caller reported that
he had stabbed her in a room of an apartment in Ogata, Nakano Ward,
just before 10:00 a.m. When police officers arrived at the scene, they
found a girl that had been stabbed in the chest. She was later taken to
the hospital, and is still conscious. As a result of his arrest, TUYU
disbanded.

On June 8, 2024, Rei, miro, and AzyuN made their final statements on
TUYU's official twitter, announcing the end of TUYU.

On November 26, 2024, Pusu announced that he would delete all of
TUYU's music on December 31st, 2024 at 23:59 (11:59 PM) JST. However,
on January 1, 2025, TUYU announced that they would not delete all of
TUYU's music due to the majority opposing it, and also dismissed Pusu
from the group, and therefore stopping the deletion of TUYU's music.
Their newest album “Under mentality” was deleted, but the other 2
albums were kept safe.

Why I love TUYU

TUYU is a j-rock band that make such amazing music. As a musician who
listens to anything as long as it's good, TUYU was a band that I had been
looking for. Its theory and musicality in the music is simply stunning and
the singer has such a beautiful voice. Not just this, but the pianist, Miro,
and the guitarist, pusu, have mastered their art in musicianship. This
causes for their music to be so complex and great. Whenever im doing
some mundane task that allows me to listen to music while performing
the task, I always listen to TUYU.
